# Venison

Venison is an offline music player for iPhone and iPad. Play music stored 
locally on your device or in iCloud Drive — no streaming, no account required.

## Features

- Plays MP3, M4A/AAC, FLAC, WAV, and AIFF files
- Browse by Songs, Albums, Artists, Genres, and Playlists
- Equalizer with 10-band control
- AirPlay support
- Siri integration
- Edit music file tags directly on your device

## Adding Music

Transfer music files to the Venison folder in the Files app on your iPhone 
or iPad (On My iPhone → Venison), or point the app at any folder in iCloud Drive 
via Settings → Music Folders → Add Folder.
