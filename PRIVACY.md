# Privacy Policy for Venison

*Last updated: May 2026*

Venison does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data 
of any kind.

- All music files remain entirely on your device
- No analytics or crash reporting
- No advertising
- No tracking
- No network connections are made by the app (AirPlay and Siri use 
  Apple's own frameworks and are subject to Apple's privacy policy)

This app has no servers and no accounts. There is nothing to collect.
